# Master AI: Beginner → Veteran Roadmap (First Principles Approach)

---

# Learning Philosophy

## First Principle

AI is fundamentally a system that learns patterns from data and uses those patterns to make predictions or generate outputs.

Everything in AI ultimately comes down to:

```text
Data
 ↓
Representation
 ↓
Learning
 ↓
Prediction
 ↓
Decision / Generation
```
